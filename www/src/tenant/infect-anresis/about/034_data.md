## Data
Anonymized antimicrobial susceptibility data is provided by the Swiss Centre for Antibiotic resistance ([anresis.ch](http://anresis.ch)). Data is provided as delivered by the participating laboratories. Data is updated on a monthly basis and contains susceptibility information for the previous 12 months.

The antimicrobial treatment guideline data are based on [guidelines from the Swiss Society for Infectious Diseases (SSI)](http://ssi.guidelines.ch).
